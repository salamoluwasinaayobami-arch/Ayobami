What to Expect

Whether you've been attending church for years or this is your first time, you'll find a warm welcome at Grace Community.

Come as You Are

There is no dress code. You'll see everything from suits to jeans. We care more about you than what you wear.

Worship Style

Our 9 AM service features traditional hymns and choir. Our 11 AM service features modern worship songs led by a band.

For the Kids

We have a safe, fun, and educational Children's Ministry for infants through 5th grade during the 11 AM service.
